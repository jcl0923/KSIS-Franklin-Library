富蘭克林數位圖書館

崑山國際學校富蘭克林圖書館，以美國第三十二任總統富蘭克林·德拉諾·羅斯福（Franklin Delano Roosevelt）命名。羅斯福總統深信教育是民主的基石，更是每個公民通往自由與平等的橋梁。本圖書館以此精神為核心，致力於為崑山國中部及高中部師生提供廣泛、多元的優質閱讀資源。

The Franklin Library at Kuen Shan International School is named in honor of Franklin Delano Roosevelt, the 32nd President of the United States. President Roosevelt believed that education is the true safeguard of democracy and the bridge between citizens and freedom. This library embodies that spirit, dedicated to providing KSIS junior high and high school students and teachers with broad, diverse, and high-quality reading resources.

本數位圖書館整合 Project Gutenberg 超過 70,000 冊公共領域電子書，涵蓋文學、歷史、科學、詩歌、哲學、傳記等各大類別，完全免費提供師生線上閱讀與下載，不受時間及地點限制。

Our digital library integrates over 70,000 public-domain e-books from Project Gutenberg, spanning literature, history, science, poetry, philosophy, biography, and more — all freely available for reading and download by KSIS students and teachers, anytime and anywhere.

IB 候選學校

崑山國際學校目前為 IB 國際文憑組織候選學校，積極推動國際化教育，培養具備全球視野、批判思考及跨文化溝通能力的未來公民。富蘭克林數位圖書館是支持 IB 課程探究學習的重要資源平台。

Kuen Shan International School is currently an IB World School candidate, committed to advancing international education and fostering globally minded, critically thinking graduates. The Franklin Digital Library serves as a key resource platform supporting IB inquiry-based learning.

使用說明

如何使用數位圖書館

1. 使用學校帳號（學號 + 密碼）登入系統。

1. Sign in using your school account (student ID + password).

2. 在書庫頁面瀏覽或搜尋感興趣的書籍，可依類別篩選。

2. Browse or search the catalog by title, author, or category.

3. 點擊書籍封面進入詳情頁面，查看書本介紹。

3. Click on a book cover to view its detail page.

4. 點選「線上閱讀」即可在瀏覽器中直接閱讀；或點選「下載 EPUB」儲存到裝置離線閱讀。

4. Click "Read Online" to read in your browser, or "Download EPUB" to save for offline reading.

5. 所有書籍均來自 Project Gutenberg，版權已進入公共領域，可免費合法取用。

5. All books are from Project Gutenberg — public domain, free, and legal to read and download.
