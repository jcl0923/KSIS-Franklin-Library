富蘭克林圖書館

Franklin Library · Kuen Shan International School

崑山國際學校富蘭克林數位圖書館以美國開國元勛班傑明·富蘭克林命名，象徵求知精神、智慧與創新。富蘭克林本人是一位博學多才的人物——科學家、發明家、作家、政治家——體現了本校對全人教育的理想。

The Franklin Digital Library of Kuen Shan International School is named after Benjamin Franklin, Founding Father and polymath, symbolizing the pursuit of knowledge, wisdom, and lifelong learning. As a scientist, inventor, writer, and statesman, Franklin embodies our school's commitment to well-rounded education.

本數位圖書館收錄超過1,200本適合國中及高中學生閱讀的中英文書籍，所有資源均為版權自由或合法授權之開放資源，學生可隨時在線上閱讀。

This library provides access to 1,200+ copyright-free and openly licensed books appropriate for junior high and high school students, in both Chinese and English.

"Tell me and I forget, teach me and I may remember, involve me and I learn." — Benjamin Franklin
